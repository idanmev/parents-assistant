## ADHD Profile

**Core deficits (Natascha's assessment):**
- Emotional dysregulation — goes 0→100 fast, slow to recover
- Working memory — forgets sequences, loses track mid-task
- Executive function — difficulty initiating, planning, task-switching
- Inhibitory control — acts before thinking, body-level limit testing
- Time perception — can't gauge how long things take; hyperfocuses without noticing time

**Sensory profile (high sensitivity):**
- Tactile: wet/damp clothing = genuinely intolerable (not stubbornness)
- Auditory: baby crying, loud environments → emotional flooding
- Olfactory: certain foods/smells require advance warning
- Social-energetic: absorbs others' presence intensely (tone, movement, energy)

**Rejection Sensitivity Dysphoria (RSD-type):**
- Disproportionate distress after mild correction
- "I'm bad" / "nobody likes me" self-talk
- Avoids activities where she might fail or be evaluated
- Anticipation of rejection is often more painful than the rejection itself
- Peer issues (friend went home from school early → Maya wanted to go home too)

## What Maya Needs (Non-Negotiable)

1. **Predictability** — she must know what happens next, always
2. **One step at a time** — sequences overwhelm her executive system
3. **Empathy before demand** — her nervous system must settle before she can comply
4. **Daily 1:1 time** with at least one parent (10–15 min, child leads)
5. **Kept promises** — this is how trust is built; broken promises cause outsized distress
6. **Specific labeled praise** — vague praise has no effect; "I saw you..." works
7. **Physical/sensory comfort** — don't dismiss sensory complaints as manipulation
8. **Clear limits paired with alternatives** — "not X, yes Y" always, never just "no"
