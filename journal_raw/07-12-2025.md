- 🔵 COACH: "אני מציעה לעשות את זה כך: בערב מכינים יחד את הבגדים, ונותנים למאיה לבחור בעצמה. כשיש לה תחושת שליטה – רמת ההתנגדות שלה יורדת משמעותית"
  - **Independence Strategy:**
  - Evening: prepare clothes together, Maya chooses herself → sense of control = resistance drops significantly

  - Reinforce for every small step: choice, effort, attempt. Reinforcement is the fuel that drives her forward in the morning.
  - **First step:** Maya chooses ONE item she puts on herself (even underwear/socks count). Helps her cross a small barrier of insecurity.
  - Important: even when Maya dresses alone, you or Idan stay beside her so she sees no one disappears when she becomes independent. Opposite — you reinforce and lift her. This is what reinforces the behavior going forward.
