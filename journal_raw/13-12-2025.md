## Saturday — Birthday Party + Race Event

### Sounds in the car
- Maya making loud sounds in the car (especially imitating Yonatan). Parents asked her to stop several times.
- 🔵 COACH: "זה לא 'הרגל רע' — הקולות שמאיה עושה הם תגובה אוטומטית של מערכת העצבים כשהיא עייפה, מוצפת או מחקה את יוני"
  - This is NOT a bad habit. These sounds are an automatic nervous system response when tired/overwhelmed — common in ADHD + high-sensitivity kids, especially during transitions.
  - Don't stop the sound by force. Help the body calm first, then set a calm limit.
  - In real-time: "Maya, I hear your body making sounds. Let's help it for a moment." → offer physical alternative (deep breath, press feet to floor, hum quietly, hold something).
  - Only after calming: "In the car we keep a quiet voice. If it's hard — I'm here to help."
  - Reinforce every attempt: "I noticed you managed to stop for a moment — that wasn't easy." → builds internal regulation + self-confidence without damaging self-image.
